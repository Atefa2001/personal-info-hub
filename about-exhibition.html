About

A Blsck and White Gallery is a special type of art exhibition that focuses on photographs without color, these galleries remove colors to highlight light, shadow, contrast, texture and emotions.

Main goals of black and white galleries emphasizing emotions and mood beacuse Without colors, the viewer pays more attention to expressions, eyes, and feelings. Focus on Composition Form, lines, patterns, and structure become more important. Creating a Nostalgic Feeling Black and white photos often remind people of older times and carry a deeper emotional impact.
